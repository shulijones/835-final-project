Picture Hanging Helper -- Prototype

Developed by Shuli Jones and Emily Caragay

Hang a picture perfectly in only five steps!

Step 1: Identify wall. We need to know where you'll be hanging the picture! Position your computer so that the webcam shows all of the wall and say "save wall".

Step 2: Capture picture. Show us what your picture looks like! Hold the picture up close to the camera so that all of it is visible and say "capture picture".

Step 3: Show hanging point. Tell us how your picture will hang! On the image of your picture below, click where the nail will go and then click save. Based on that information, and what you'll tell us in Step 4 about where the picture goes on the wall, we'll calculate the nail's location.

Step 4: Show hanging location. Show us where your picture will hang! Hold your picture up to the wall right where you want it to go. Then say "save location". Then, come back here to match up your original picture to its location.
